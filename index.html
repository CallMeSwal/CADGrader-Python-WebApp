About

  • About CADGrader

    CADGrader is a software service that automatically marks CADFiles. Our software can accurate determine how similar two 3D models are. CADGraders works with Autodesk Inventor, Solid Edge, SolidWorks, and other CAD softwares.

  • Mass Marking Services

    CADGrader can quickly mark thousands CAD files against a given answer file. All students grades are automatically uploaded to a secure online database.

  • Student Marking Services

    CADGrader offers an online marking tool for students developing their CAD skills. CADGrader has a large library of CAD drawings that students can attempt modelling. Students can upload their attempts to CADGrader and have their files instantly marked.
